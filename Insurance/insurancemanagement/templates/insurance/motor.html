BIMA BAMBA MOTOR COVER

Your first payment is made up of 1 month Comprehensive cover and an annual Third Party cover. The subsequent 11 monthly payments are for the remaining Comprehensive cover.We will send you reminders when a payment is due. You will have a grace period of 3 days from the payment date to meet your obligation. We want to make sure you are always covered; so failure to make a payment will result in your cover being downgraded to Third Party.
